The Live Demo Floor
40+
The Machines
Are The Event.

The demo floor is not an exhibition hall. It is a curated showcase of working autonomous systems operating in real time — the only place in the world where you can walk between 40+ live robots in a single afternoon.

For investors, it is due diligence made visceral. For enterprise buyers, it is procurement made physical. For researchers, it is the state of the art, live.

The live demo floor creates sponsorship inventory unavailable at any other event. A robotics company demonstrating a working system to 6,000 senior buyers and investors in one room cannot replicate this outcome through any other channel.
Humanoid Systems
Figure AI, Agility Robotics, 1X Technologies, Boston Dynamics, Apptronik, and Unitree on an open floor.
Industrial & Warehouse

Autonomous Vehicles
Self-driving platforms, last-mile delivery robots, drone systems from Waymo, Zipline, Skydio.
